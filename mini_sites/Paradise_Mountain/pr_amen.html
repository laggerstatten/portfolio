Our Amenities

Paradise Garden Day Spa

Paradise Garden Day Spa combines a tranquil and picturesque environment, with a highly trained, friendly staff and superb services to ensure you have a memorable and enjoyable spa experience.

Paradise Garden offers an array of spa treatments for the utmost in relaxation and rejuvenation as well as a variety of full-body and point-relaxation massage treatments, skincare, hydrotherapy and other spa services.

Enjoy one of our relaxing massages or spend the day relaxing in one of our two outdoor hot tubs in a luxurious garden setting.

To schedule an appointment phone:

1.970.555.7372 or dial EXT 7372 from your room

Tennis Facility

Come play at our recently remodeled tennis courts. Visit the pro shop for modern equipment and coaching. If the weather's bad outside, check out our indoor facilities.

There's always a partner to play with, and our staff of tennis pros can help you improve your game.

To reserve a court phone:

1.970.555.5375 or dial EXT 5375 from your room

Snowshoe Trails

Try any of our hiking and snowshoe trails. Go solo, with a partner, or on one of our guided group tours. Experiences range from a casual half-mile around the lodge to our challenging ten-mile expert trail.

Check out equipment at the outdoor center, but don't stop at snowshoes. Grab a pair of binoculars to get a look at our native Rockies birds. Guided tours leave every morning 30 minutes before sunrise, so you can experience a true mountain sun up.

To sign up for a tour phone:

1.970.555.5375 or dial EXT 5375 from your room

Kayaking

Come take a trip down the river in a one-person or two-person kayak. Our beautiful mountain scenery is sure to inspire anyone. Experienced kayakers may want to take the overnight trip and camp at our landing 20 miles downriver.

Equipment is available from the outdoor center. New kayakers may want to take a kayak class from out pro trainer before setting out. If you're staying with us over the first weekend of the month, you can join the local kayak club for their river regatta.

To rent a kayak phone:

1.970.555.5375 or dial EXT 5375 from your room
